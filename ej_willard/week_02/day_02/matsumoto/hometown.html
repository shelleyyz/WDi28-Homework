まつもとゆきひろ

Hometown

Matsumoto was born in Osaka Prefecture, Japan, on 14 April 1965. At age four he moved to Tottori Prefecture.

According to an interview conducted by Japan Inc., he was a self-taught programmer until the end of high school. He graduated with an information science degree from University of Tsukuba, where he was a member of Ikuo Nakata's research lab on programming languages and compilers.

Quick facts about Hometown

Osaka is Japan's third largest city.

Osaka is known as the 'nation's kitchen'. It's most famous dished are おこのみやき (Okonomiyaki - savoury pancake and たこやき　(tako-yaki - savoury pancake balls with octopus inside)

Osaka or おさか. means 'large hill'

Tottori Prefecture is a rural prefecture that is home to some famous sand dunes and is the least populated of all the prefectures in Japan.
